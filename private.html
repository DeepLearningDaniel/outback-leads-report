What is this?

Daniel built an AI-powered lead gen tool that automatically searches private school websites across Texas to find the right people to contact about team building programs and Texas Adventure Camp (Summer 2026: June 21-27 & July 26-Aug 1, ages 8-17, $1,150).

This report covers 158 private schools within ~4 hours of the Ranch. Found 508 contacts — 472 with email addresses ready to go.

How to use: Tap any email to copy it, then paste into your email and attach your pitch.

Who are these people & why they matter

Heads of School / Principals
Top decision makers. Control budgets, approve programs, set school culture. #1 target.
Athletic Directors
Book team camps, pre-season retreats, athletic bonding. Big budget holders.
Admissions Directors
Market to prospective families. Value enrichment programs they can promote.
Counselors & Activity Coordinators
Recommend camps to families, plan field trips, organize outdoor education.
